About VidSync

We help creators publish smarter

VidSync is a video scheduling and publishing platform built to help content creators and social media teams automate their TikTok publishing workflow using TikTok's official Content Posting API.

We started VidSync because we saw how much time creators were losing to manual publishing — logging in at the right hour, formatting captions, checking hashtags, and then repeating it all the next day. The actual creative work was getting squeezed out by operational overhead.

Our mission is simple: let creators focus on making great video content, and let VidSync handle the scheduling and publishing. By integrating directly with TikTok's official Content Posting API, VidSync provides a reliable, policy-compliant way to automate video publishing without relying on unofficial workarounds or third-party scraping.

VidSync operates strictly within TikTok's developer guidelines. We only request the minimum necessary API permissions to schedule and publish videos on behalf of users who have explicitly authorized our application. We never access, store, or share user data beyond what is required to deliver our service.

TikTok API Integration

VidSync uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish videos on behalf of authorized users. Our integration scope is limited to video upload and post creation. We do not access TikTok Direct Messages, user contact lists, or any data outside the specific permissions granted by the user during OAuth authorization.

All API usage follows TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and Content Sharing Guidelines. If you have questions about our API integration or data handling practices, please see our Privacy Policy or contact us directly.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

VidSync ("we," "us," or "our") operates the VidSync platform, which provides video scheduling and publishing services via the TikTok Content Posting API. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services.

By using VidSync, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you register for VidSync or use our services, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Video content: Video files, captions, hashtags, and scheduling metadata you upload to be published via TikTok's API.
  • Communications: Messages you send us via our contact form or email support.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use VidSync, we may automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and timestamps.
  • Usage data: Features used, actions taken in the dashboard, and publishing activity.
  • Cookies: Session cookies to maintain your login state and preferences. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

1.3 TikTok OAuth Data

When you connect your TikTok account to VidSync via OAuth, we receive and store:

  • Your TikTok User ID (an anonymous identifier, not your username).
  • An access token and refresh token, which we use solely to publish videos on your behalf.
  • Basic profile information made available by TikTok's API (display name, profile image URL).

We do not access your TikTok messages, contacts, followers list, or any data outside the specific scopes you authorize during the OAuth flow. We request only the minimum permissions required to provide our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To operate the platform, process your video uploads, schedule posts, and publish them to TikTok via the API.
  • Account management: To create and maintain your account, authenticate your sessions, and manage your preferences.
  • Customer support: To respond to your inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and improve service quality.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to your account.
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and TikTok's developer policies.
  • Product improvement: To analyze aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to improve VidSync's features and performance.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • TikTok: When you schedule a post, we transmit your video file and post metadata to TikTok's servers via their official API. This transmission is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy.
  • Service providers: We may engage third-party service providers for hosting, storage, and analytics. These providers are contractually bound to process data only on our behalf and in accordance with this policy.
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your information may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to the same protections described here.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@vidsync.app.

Video files uploaded to VidSync are deleted from our servers within 30 days of the scheduled publish date. Access tokens for TikTok are automatically purged when you disconnect your TikTok account.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Access tokens and credentials are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • We conduct regular security assessments of our infrastructure.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Disconnect your TikTok account at any time to revoke our API access.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@vidsync.app.

7. Cookies

VidSync uses only essential cookies necessary to operate the service. These include session cookies to keep you logged in and preference cookies to remember your settings. We do not use advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking cookies.

You may configure your browser to refuse cookies, but doing so may impact your ability to use VidSync's authenticated features.

8. Children's Privacy

VidSync is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

VidSync is operated from Indonesia. If you access our services from outside Indonesia, your information may be transferred to and processed in Indonesia or other countries where our servers or service providers are located. By using VidSync, you consent to this transfer.
